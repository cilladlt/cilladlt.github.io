About Pricilla

Pricilla Delatorre is a 2D concept artist specializing in telling stories through her designs. Pricilla paints her worlds by creating physical and digital sketches, then tying together a combination of vector art, digital painting, and texturing. Her favorite tools are Sketchbook Pro, Illustrator, and Photoshop.
